Overview

Purpose

My sister and I want to build up a website to promote our hand-made cakes, bread and dessert. With the rise of online platforms and social media, targeting individuals who are active online can be beneficial. Engage with potential customers by sharing visually appealing cake photos, and posting tutorials or behind-the-scenes content. Through a website and online platforms to establish an online presence for visitors. They can contact us and leave us the message by using the website. As we are still testing our business by an online shore, it seems to be a good choice for us to promote our products. No need to find a location, rent a place and hire employee. We can open a new business at a reasonable cost on the internet and our guests can use the lower price to buy our products.

Audience

We want to focus on individuals who are celebrating special occasions. They are looking for customized cakes for events such as birthdays, engagements, anniversaries, graduations, baby showers, and other milestone celebrations. We also target families and individuals who enjoy home celebrations and smaller gatherings, such as family reunions, housewarming, and holidays. On the other hand, we welcome guests to order their own special cakes according to their special needs. For example, individuals who have a food allergy to egg and milk. We can make a special cake for them.

Wireframes

Create three wireframes for your site. One for each page and list them here

Home

The header shows our company logo and company name. We use a Lion color for the background of the header. We use gray color for the text. We want to create a feeling in the bakery. Then the next line shows the link for Home, Gallery, and Contact because the first line shows the company name. We do not have space to display the bottom in the first line. Then I would like to put the company's news or promotion or latest selling cake in the big image area. Under the new and promotion section, we show the main category of our product. They are bread, cake, and dessert. Next, we give a welcome message to visitors. Then we point our selling point on the Home Page. Our main selling point is catering to different dietary needs. We mainly use the fresh fruit to make the cake. I will put the fresh food or organic food photos here. For the welcome message’s photo and the selling point’s photo, we will place a color background under it. The lower side is the footer. We use blue color for the background and white color for the text. On the footer, it will show the company name again and display the icons which are IG, Facebook, email, and WhatsApp. I would like to set the link for these icons. We hope that our visitors can directly click the icon to contact us or check out our social media.

Gallery

For the page of Gallery's header and footer, we use the same design as the Home Page. The header shows our company logo and company name in a Lion color. On the footer, it will show the company name again and display the icons which are IG, Facebook, email, and WhatsApp in blue background color. Then the next line shows the link for Home, Gallery, and Contact. There is some special of Gallery. I would like to add the Image Scroller in the big image area. It displays our top five or top ten cake, bread, and dessert photos. The lower area displays our products. We will put different photos in different categories in different areas. According to Visitor’s special needs, they can find the specific corner for their special cake, especially for weddings, anniversaries, graduations, and baby showers. We are still collecting the comments on our product. This page includes many photos. I will put the photos after I resize the photo size so that the visitor can open it easily. I will type brief product information under the photo, such as product name, amount, size, and any special allergenic materials. We will show it later on this page. As we want the visitor can see our product clearly, we will use a white background color.

Contact

The header shows our company logo and company name. We use a Lion color for the background of the header. We use gray color for the text. We want to create a feeling in the bakery. Then the next line shows the link for Home, Gallery, and Contact because the first line shows the company name. We do not have space to display the bottom in the first line. The lower side is the footer. We use blue color for the background and white color for the text. On the footer, it will show the company name again and display the icons which are IG, Facebook, email, and WhatsApp. At this moment we only show the contact, including the email address, official working hours, and the phone number. It is because our shop is still an online shop. If the online store can break even, we will consider renting a store. In that time, we will show the map on it. And set up more pages for the website. For example, the order page, About us page, and FAQ page.
